# AnonMafia Cyber Family (hacker group)

**AnonMafia Cyber Family** , also known as **AMCF** or
**AnonMafia** , is an hacktivist collective operating as an
offshoot of the Anonymous hacking collective. The group
formed on Facebook in September 2014 (originally as
**AnonMafia** ) during the 2014 Hong Kong protests, they were
outraged by the treatment thus response from the Hong Kong
government and local authorities towards the protesting
students, this led them to their first cyber operation known as
#OpHongKong, which they later contacted an independent
journalist known as Ford Fischer (Founder of News2Share) to
help spread notoriety of the operation, this minor
collaboration led to the first video ever, of Anonymous
addressing the protest in Hong Kong (The video no longer
exist nor can be found).

On October 3 2014, they decided to move their cyber
operations to twitter in hopes to reach a broader audience,
leading to a change of their name from **AnonMafia** to
**AnonMafia Cyber Family** and also merged with another
group called **Ghost Security Anonymous (GSA)** who
became adopted under the **AMCF** umbrella, they eventually
tied themselves into a new up and coming operation known as
#OpKKK, they later re-named #OpTakkk for themselves.
During the operation, they launched a multitude amount of
Denial-of-service attack against numerous KKK-linked
websites, including a website belonging to the **Traditionalist American Knights of the Ku Klux Klan
(TAK),** a white supremacist hate group, largely based in Missouri and active during the 2010s, which was
founded and led by Imperial Wizard _Frank Ancona_ (later murdered in 2017). Who they later doxed and
released his information to the public. Their involvement with this operation put **AnonMafia Cyber
Family** on the map in the Anonymous (hacker group) community.

**AnonMafia Cyber Family** released a large amount of doxing information on local law enforcement in
Missouri, New York City, Ferguson, and Kansas City during Operation Ferguson, Operation Kendrick
Johnson, and Operation BaltimorePD to the public. As well as launching crucial Denial-of-service attacks
against government websites as protest against treatment of the Kendrick Johnson case and Freddie Grey. - #OpFerguson #OpKendrickJohnson #OpBaltimorePD #OpBayOfPigs

**AnonMafia Cyber Family** targeted the swedish government as payback for Swedish law enforcement
seizing Pirate Bay servers resulting in the permanent closure of the Pirate Bay platform. - #OpPirateBay

**AnonMafia Cyber Family** hacked into and defaced ISIS-linked websites, as well as launched numerous
Denial-of-service attacks to ISIS-linked Websites and services, and accused cloudflare on multiple
occasions of protecting ISIS-linked websites hosted on their platform. Forcing ISIS-linked website users
to retreat to Telegram for communication. - #OpCharlieHebdo #OpISIS #OpParis

**AnonMafia Cyber Family** launched numerous Denial-of-service attacks against websites that contained
Bestiality content, as well as reporting the sites to their hosting providers, resulting in the termination of
hundreds of sites, also accusing a chicago-based company FDC Servers (https://fdcservers.net/) of
allowing Bestiality websites to be hosted on their platform. - #OpBEAST

**AnonMafia Cyber Family** hacked into and defaced Chinese government websites, as well as launched
numerous Denial-of-service attacks to Chinese government websites and services, to protest the Yulin
Dog Meat Festival. - #OpStopYuLin #OpChina

**AnonMafia Cyber Family** launched numerous Denial-of-service attacks against Israeli government
websites and services, alongside AnonGhost and Middle East Cyber Army (MECA) to protest against
Zionism and unruly treatment of Palestinians. - #OpIsrael
